The SAAB Stories Archive


This page is the spiritual successor to a community snapchat story I started after leaving Tufts in the Spring 0f 2019, archiving a series of poorly-planned yet masterfully executed cross-country expeditions across the United States, including contributions from some of the friends I made along the way. SAAB Stories features 60 different accounts from 38 US States and spans the transition between two different SAAB cars. For almost two years, I've been thinking of better ways to turn these memories into something more permanent and substantive than the old platform while still being able to include those multiple perspectives that made the original Saab Stories so special. This work-in-progress website is my first attempt at making that happen. Thank you for stopping in.
